Shared Values

At the National Research Institute for Democratized Learning (NRIDL), our shared values guide how we work, learn, and collaborate. They shape our culture, our relationships, and the impact we create. These principles inform our efforts to ensure that digital and AI-driven opportunities are accessible to all.

1

Upholding Excellence, Integrity, and Empathy

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, approaching our research, community engagements, and partnerships with honesty, fairness, and a deep respect for others. By holding ourselves accountable and listening with empathy, we foster trust and transparency in all interactions.

2

Championing Inclusivity and Community

We believe that true innovation springs from diversity. We are committed to creating an environment where people of all identities, backgrounds, and perspectives can thrive. By celebrating differences and nurturing an inclusive digital culture, we ensure that everyone's voice can influence and shape the future.

3

Fostering Equitable Learning and Innovation

As we democratize access to AI and digital tools, we embrace creativity, curiosity, and bold thinking. We encourage risk-taking within ethical and responsible boundaries, recognizing that new solutions often emerge from challenging assumptions. Our goal is to help individuals and communities not only adapt, but lead, in a rapidly evolving digital world.

4

Collaborating Openly for Transformative Impact

We believe that solving complex global challenges requires open dialogue and partnerships that transcend traditional boundaries. We actively seek out collaborative opportunities with academia, industry, governments, nonprofits, and local communities. By sharing knowledge, resources, and ideas, we amplify our collective impact and move closer to a more just, inclusive, and sustainable future.

Unlock Generative AI Safely and Responsibly

As with any emerging technology, it is essential to educate and empower each one of us to use generative AI responsibly. Digital literacy skills and online safety principles are vital for helping us navigate these tools effectively and build resilience in the digital space.

Microsoft's Classroom Toolkit: Unlocking Generative AI Safely and Responsibly is an innovative resource designed for educators and students aged 13-15. It combines engaging narrative stories with practical instructional content to create an immersive learning experience.

The toolkit supports educators in fostering meaningful discussions about responsible AI practices in the classroom. Key topics include recognizing content fabrications, understanding privacy concerns, addressing bias, and promoting mental wellbeing.
